Taniti City:

Photo by Hilthart Pedersen on Unsplash

Taniti City is home to native architecture preserved in the historic district where the culture and heritage still lives. Local guides are eager to share their island's rich culture.

Yellow Leaf Bay apart of Taniti City's coast notable for it's soft white sand beaches. Guests can enjoy soaking up the sun, swimming, walking the shore or watching the tide role in. Cabanas are available for rent with table side service.

Boat and bus tours are available for guests looking to adventure. These tours afford the guests a chance to see the island's diverse landscapes, from the soft sandy beaches to the volcanic Rainforest. Local guides are excited to share their interesting facts and stories about the island.

Taniti City offers a variety of activities, including historical exploration, beach relaxation, and adventure tours. There is something for everyone to enjoy.

Merriton Landing:

The island museum is staffed by locals that know the historical culture of the island. artifacts, exhibits, and live interactive demonstration are featured in the island museum

The Island is home to several pubs offering our local brews, traditional island music, live entertainment.

For beer enthusiasts Merriton Landing has a microbrewery that supplies our island pubs. It features a variety of craft beers sourced with local ingredients. Tours and tastings are available for booking during your stay.

Join us for an electrifying night at the grand opening of Taniti's newest dance club! It's going to be an evening full of lively music, captivating performances, and an unbeatable vibe.

On the Landing we have a state-of-the-art theater equipped with lazy boy heated recliners and Dolby Cinema powerful image-and-sound technologies.

Available for booking is our helicopter tours. These tours offer a view of Taniti from the skies. fly by the beaches, our lush rainforest, and our active volcano.

Next to our pubs at the landing is our classic arcade, fun destination for all ages. It offers a variety of games from classic pinball machines to the latest arcade video games.

Visitors to the island can enjoy our Art galleries featuring works from local artist hosting a diverse range of styles and mediums. Visitors are welcome to purchase unique pieces that capture the culture of the island.

Beach and Sea:

Photo by Natasha Jenny on Unsplash

Offered at our shores for booking is our chartered fishing trips. Spend the day out on the water with our experienced local guides. Food and drink is included during this tour.

Also offered is our snorkeling tour of the closest coral reef filled with colorful fish. Food and drink are offered during your tour of the reef.

Lounge at the beach for free. watch the tide role away or stop by the beach side bar for a refreshing island drink.

Rainforest:

The Island has a lush rainforest home to exotic flora and fauna. Soar above the trees and get an exhilarating view zip-lining through the rainforest.

Hiking trails curated by local rangers are available to discover the local flora and fauna. This is one of many free actives provided by the island for visitors to explore. Stop by our visitor center to pickup your safety and informational brochure and stay hydrated.

Volcano:

Photo by Conor Murphy on Unsplash

Passed the rainforest in the center of the Island is our active volcano. Book your experience to get a guided tour of the volcano and learn the geological history of the island.

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. While the island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000. Until a recent increase in tourism, most of the Tanitian economy was dominated by fishing or agriculture.
